Sponsorship & Advertising Opportunities

Ferris State E-Sports offers a variety of opportunities for businesses and organizations to sponsor and advertise with us. Whether you are looking to promote your brand or product to our dedicated fanbase, or support the growth of e-sports as a whole, we have options that will fit your needs and budget.

Sponsorship Packages

Our sponsorship packages offer a range of benefits to our sponsors, including:

  • Logo placement on our website and social media pages
  • Recognition in our live streams and video content
  • Opportunities for product giveaways and promotions
  • Access to our players and coaches for promotional events

For more information on our sponsorship packages, please contact us at sponsorship@ferrisstateesports.com.

Advertising Opportunities

We offer a variety of advertising opportunities, including:

  • Ad placements on our website and social media pages
  • Sponsored posts on our social media pages
  • Product placement in our live streams and video content

For more information on our advertising opportunities, please contact us at advertising@ferrisstateesports.com.
