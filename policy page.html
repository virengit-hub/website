Code of Conduct

At Ferris State e-Sports, we expect all members to conduct themselves in a professional and respectful manner at all times. This includes but is not limited to the following:

  • Respecting the rights and dignity of all individuals
  • Avoiding discriminatory language or behavior
  • Avoiding any form of cheating or exploitation in games
  • Using appropriate language and behavior in team communications
  • Representing the organization in a positive manner at all times

Membership Requirements

To be a member of Ferris State e-Sports, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Be a current student at Ferris State University
  • Be in good academic standing with the university
  • Abide by the organization's code of conduct
  • Attend meetings and events as required by the organization

Disciplinary Action

Violation of the organization's code of conduct may result in disciplinary action, up to and including suspension or expulsion from the organization. All disciplinary action will be decided upon by the executive board and may be appealed to the general membership.
